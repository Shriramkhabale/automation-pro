Privacy Policy for BizConnect Pro

Last Updated: May 8, 2026

Introduction

BizConnect Pro ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Phone Number: Used for authentication and account identification
  • Name: For personalizing your experience
  • Contact List: To help you manage business contacts and leads
  • Call Logs: To provide call tracking and analytics features
  • Device Information: Device ID, operating system, app version

Usage Data

  • Call Statistics: Incoming, outgoing, missed, and rejected call data
  • Message History: Count of messages sent (not content)
  • Lead Information: Business leads you manually add to the app
  • Follow-up Reminders: Reminder data you create
  • Subscription Status: Payment and subscription information

Payment Information

  • Payment data is processed securely through Razorpay
  • We do not store complete credit card or payment details
  • Only transaction IDs and subscription status are stored

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

  1. Core Functionality

    • Authenticate and identify your account
    • Track and analyze your business calls
    • Manage leads and follow-up reminders
    • Send messages on your behalf (with your explicit action)
    • Create marketing materials (posters, frames, digital cards)

  2. Service Improvement

    • Analyze app usage to improve features
    • Provide customer support
    • Send important service notifications

  3. Subscription Management

    • Process payments through Razorpay
    • Manage subscription status and expiry
    • Prevent unauthorized access

Data Storage and Security

Storage Location

  • Data is stored on Firebase (Google Cloud Platform)
  • Servers located in secure data centers
  • Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS

Security Measures

  • Firebase Authentication for secure login
  • Session management to prevent unauthorized access
  • Device-specific session tokens
  • Regular security updates

Data Retention

  • Active user data: Retained while account is active
  • Deleted account data: Removed within 30 days of deletion request
  • Call logs: Retained for analytics purposes while account is active
  • Payment records: Retained as required by law (minimum 7 years)

Data Sharing and Disclosure

Third-Party Services

We share limited data with:

  1. Firebase (Google)

    • Purpose: Authentication, database, cloud storage
    • Data shared: Phone number, user data, app usage
    • Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

  2. Razorpay

    • Purpose: Payment processing
    • Data shared: Payment information, transaction details
    • Privacy Policy: https://razorpay.com/privacy/

  3. Firebase Cloud Messaging

    • Purpose: Push notifications for reminders
    • Data shared: Device tokens, notification content
    • Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

We Do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your contact list with advertisers
  • Access your WhatsApp messages or content
  • Share your call logs with external parties
  • Use your data for purposes other than stated

Permissions Explained

Why We Need Each Permission

Phone State (READ_PHONE_STATE)

  • Detect incoming and outgoing calls for call tracking
  • Identify call duration and call type
  • Essential for call management features

SMS (SEND_SMS)

  • Send SMS messages when you explicitly trigger message sending
  • Used only when you activate SMS features
  • You control when messages are sent

Contacts (READ_CONTACTS, WRITE_CONTACTS)

  • Display contact names in call logs
  • Save new contacts from calls
  • Manage your business leads
  • All contact access is local to your device

Call Log (READ_CALL_LOG)

  • Analyze call history for business insights
  • Track missed calls and follow-ups
  • Generate call statistics and reports

Storage/Media (READMEDIA*, WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE)

  • Save created posters and frames
  • Access images for marketing materials
  • Store digital cards and business content

Camera

  • Take photos for frames and posters
  • Capture images for digital cards
  • Used only when you activate camera features

Accessibility Service (BIND_ACCESSIBILITY_SERVICE)

  • IMPORTANT: This permission is used to assist with message composition
  • Helps users with repetitive messaging tasks
  • You must explicitly enable this feature
  • Can be disabled at any time in Android Settings

Foreground Service

  • Keep call detection running in background
  • Ensure follow-up reminders work reliably
  • Maintain app functionality when not actively using

Battery Optimization

  • Prevent Android from stopping call detection
  • Ensure timely follow-up notifications
  • Optional but recommended for best experience

Your Rights and Choices

Access Your Data

  • View all your data in the app's Profile section
  • Export your leads and call data (contact support)

Delete Your Data

  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Go to Settings > Profile > Delete Account
  • Or contact us at support@bizconnectpro.app

Control Permissions

  • Revoke any permission in Android Settings > Apps > BizConnect Pro > Permissions
  • Disable features you don't use
  • Turn off SMS/WhatsApp features in app Settings

Opt-Out Options

  • Disable call tracking in Settings
  • Turn off message sending features
  • Disable push notifications
  • Stop using accessibility service
